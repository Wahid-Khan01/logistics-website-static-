Air Freight Logistic Services

Air freight is a shipping method that involves the transportation of goods by aircraft. This service is ideal for businesses needing fast delivery, high-value cargo, or shipments to locations not easily accessible by other means.

Benefits of Air Freight:

  • Speed: Air freight is the fastest mode of transport, making it ideal for urgent shipments.
  • Reliability: With frequent flight schedules, air freight offers reliable delivery times.
  • Security: Air freight is considered one of the most secure shipping methods, with stringent regulations and handling procedures.
  • Global Reach: Air freight can reach virtually any destination worldwide, making it suitable for international shipping.

How Air Freight Works:

In air freight shipping, goods are transported from the shipper's location to the airport by truck. The cargo is then loaded onto an aircraft and flown to the destination airport.

At the destination airport, the cargo goes through customs clearance before being delivered to the consignee's location by truck or other means.

Types of Cargo Suitable for Air Freight:

Air freight is suitable for a variety of cargo types, including:

  • Time-sensitive shipments
  • High-value goods
  • Perishable items
  • Medical supplies
  • Small to medium-sized packages

The Air Freight Shipping Process:

  1. Booking: The shipper books space with an air freight carrier.
  2. Pickup: Goods are picked up from the shipper's location and transported to the airport.
  3. Air Transport: Cargo is loaded onto an aircraft and flown to the destination airport.
  4. Customs Clearance: At the destination, the cargo goes through customs clearance procedures.
  5. Delivery: Once cleared, the cargo is delivered to the consignee's location.
